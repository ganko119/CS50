Tomoyuki Kambe

Tomoyuki Kambe

I came across his works at the Xmas Art Festa in Ginza, December 2016. His paintings were expensive for me, that time. I was not sure whether to buy one or not, when I returned after visiting another gallery, I found his works have sold out. I was so disappointed. I could not see his work since, because he had been working on a big project.

I met his works again at Art Fair Tokyo 2019. I finally welcomed his work into my home, then.
