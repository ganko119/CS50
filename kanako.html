Kanako Ozawa

Kanako Ozawa

I came across her works at Art Fair Tokyo 2019 in March 2019. At that time, her paintings which I liked have already sold, so I decided to wait for the fair next year. However, due to the coronavirus, Art Fair Tokyo 2020 was canceled.

I bought one of her paintings at Art Fair Tokyo 2021. I was finally able to escort her painting to my home.
